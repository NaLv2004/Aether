\section{Conclusion}
This paper presented a Joint Quantization-Aware Training (QAT) framework for Cell-Free Massive MIMO systems. By integrating a triple-head policy network at the APs with a Mean-Field GNN-Transformer detector at the CPU, we achieved efficient task-oriented fronthaul compression (representing a 29$\times$ reduction in load) while maintaining high detection accuracy. Our numerical evaluations, averaged over diverse geometric scenarios, demonstrate that the proposed framework is remarkably robust to CSI errors, pilot contamination, and AP link failures, maintaining operational reliability in challenging environments where classical linear methods fail. This approach provides a scalable and robust solution for the deployment of 6G distributed architectures under practical fronthaul constraints.
